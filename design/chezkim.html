Chez Kim

This piece was inspired by many of those restaurants nestled in newly gentrified, formerly industrial neighborhoods. Their farm-to-table menu is very expensive, low in protein, and metabolizes far too quickly. The interior is super white and rustic, and they have a chalkboard filled with illegible and calligraphed menu items. Welcome to Chez Kim
